Events

We have regular events at the Science Museum, as well as unique exhibitions and group activities.

You are welcome to explore the Museum at your own leisure, or to book an event or a guide for a specific time to get he exact experience you are looking for.

Can we trust in AI?

Artificial Intelligence is growing rapidly, and it can be used to manipulate pictures and videos within seconds.

When we mainly live our lives digitally, and are tethered to our phones - how can we know what to trust, ans how can we know how to best use this new technology?

This event includes AI-workshops.
