About

The Science Museum is a modern and innovative center of technology. This is where you come to learn, explore and experience the power of electricity, technology and artificial intelligence.

To better understand where we are going, we need to understand where we came from. At the Science Museum, you will be able to learn about how technology and creativity have affected - and is still affecting - our human world.

Enjoy a gouided tour, explore the exibits, and take part in our renovned events.

Get ready to find your SPARK - and to be amazed at what the human mind have been able to conquer.

The Science Cafè

The Science Cafè has more than just some tea, coffee and a couple of sanwiches. The café itself has a modern and cool atmosphere, and the food is out of this world - literally.

Why not test some of the space food? Food innovated by NASA to be served in outer space - simply served in our cefé instead.

Or, why not try one of our cool drinks with dry ice?

The café, of course, has food for everyone - be that if you need something glutenfree, vegan or halal. No neew to worry - we have what you need.

The Science Café is open whenever the Museum is open.
