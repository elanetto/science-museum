Technology

Modern technology, as we know it - is still new. You might have grown up with it at your fingertips, and it might seem like it has always been there - but only a few decades ago, life was quite different.

When we talk about technology at The Science Museum - we are specifically talking about modern technology from tha last 100 years. So much has happened in only a few lifetimes, that it would be almost unthinkable for someone in the 15th century How fast evolution, innovation and technology has grown in our century.

Explore our exhibits about modern technology to better understand where we came from, how we got here - and where we are going.

Maybe YOU are the key to tomorrows technology?

Technology & Coding

Airplane simulator

The Wright Brothers created their first airborne airplane in 1903. They were perceived as crazy in their time, because they believed that they could fly. Guess who's laughing now?

At The Science Museum we have several flight simulator experiences - from the first airplanes to the latest ones. Would you like to try to fly, or at least experience what it would feel like?

Try our airplane simulators at the Technology exhibitions!
