Welcome to exploration

The Science Museum welcomes you to explore the powers of the universe, and the knowledge and technology that has evolved throughout the ages. The curiosity and creativity of people has resulted in the technology we know and love today.

To better understand the future, we need to understand the past - and we need to have a deeper understanding of the technology of today. Who knows - maybe YOU will be the next explorer to figure out the innovative ideas of tomorrow?
